About Me

I believe technology's true power is its ability to transform — but only if everyone is included.

Born and raised in Seattle, Washington, I'm a senior at the University of Washington majoring in Informatics with a focus on Human-Computer Interaction (HCI). My passion is leading teams to build impactful solutions by weaving universal design and diverse perspectives into the very first thread.

My background in Informatics provided a broad technical foundation, but it was in UX that I learned to apply human-centered principles to create meaningful impact. I aspire to be a designer who bridges human needs with technical possibilities to build intuitive and inclusive digital products.
